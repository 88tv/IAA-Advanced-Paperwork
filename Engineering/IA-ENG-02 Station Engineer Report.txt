[color=#993333][head=3]Station Engineer[/head][/color]

[ ] 6. [color=#f39f27]Engineers[/color] failed to constantly monitor the Supermatter Engine.
[ ] 7. [color=#f39f27]Engineers[/color] failed to report to a hull breach within 15 minutes. 
[ ] 9. [color=#f39f27]Engineers[/color] failed to maintain the integrity of the station's power network.
